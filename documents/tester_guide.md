# 🎵 TikTok Creator Intelligence — Tester Guide

Hi! Thank you for testing my app 💙

**What is this?**
TikTok Creator Intelligence helps small creators understand their
audience. You upload your comment data, and the app analyzes how your
audience feels (sentiment), what they talk about (keywords), which of
your content types performs best, and what your viewers are literally
asking you to make next. Think of it as turning your comment section
into a content strategy.

**What you need:**
- The app link: ___________________________
- Your two data files (I sent them to you):
  - `YOURNAME_synthetic_data.csv`  → your **comments** file
  - `YOURNAME_synthetic_videos.csv` → your **videos** file

It takes about 10–15 minutes. Please go through the tasks **in order**,
and note anything confusing — your honest feedback is the whole point!

---

## Task 1 — Register

Open the app link and click **Register**. Create an account with any
username and password (use your real TikTok handle when asked — it
shows up on your profile page later). Then log in.

## Task 2 — Upload your data

Go to **Upload Data** in the left menu and follow the steps on screen:

1. Upload your **comments** CSV first (`..._synthetic_data.csv`)
2. Then upload your **videos** CSV (`..._synthetic_videos.csv`) —
   this unlocks the deeper insights, so don't skip it
3. Click **Run Analysis** and wait for the green success message

## Task 3 — Explore the Dashboard

Click **Dashboard** in the left menu. Look around:
- How does your audience feel overall? (the donut chart)
- What words stand out in your comments? (Top Keywords)
- Read a few of the example positive and negative comments —
  do they look correctly sorted to you?

## Task 4 — Check the Insights

Click **Insights**. This page groups your keywords by what your
audience is trying to say — praise, complaints, requests, or general
chat. Do the groups make sense for YOUR content?

## Task 5 — Read your Recommendations ⭐ (the important one)

Click **Recommendations**. This is the heart of the app. Check:
- Which content type does it tell you to focus on — and does the
  reasoning (the numbers) convince you?
- Look at the video ideas: are these things your audience would
  really ask for?
- Ask yourself honestly: **would you change your next video based on
  this page?**

## Task 6 — Visit your Profile

Click **User Profile**. It shows your detected niche, a content health
score, your most active commenters, and your most-liked comment.
Does the detected niche match what you actually post?
